Attack on Titan is a dark fantasy anime series where humanity is on the brink of extinction, forced to live within three concentric walls to protect themselves from giant, humanoid creatures called Titans. Death Note
Death Note is a psychological thriller anime that follows Light Yagami, a high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook that allows him to kill anyone by writing their name in it. As he uses the Death Note to rid the world of criminals, he attracts the attention of a brilliant detective known only as L, leading to a high-stakes game of cat and mouse between the two.
